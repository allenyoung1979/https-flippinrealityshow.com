What's this event all about?

When I was about 15 years old I remember my father telling me “Buy land! They’re not making any more of it” Did I buy land, or do anything in real estate? No. …Why? Partly because of what he said next. “I’ve always wanted to” he said, “Because everyone one I see whose well off is, but no one will ever tell me or show me how to go about it”. He said “It’s like some big secret no one wants to share”. Well that clearly told me, “ok why bother trying if no one’s gonna teach me”, and even when I saw someone who was sharing, I just figured well that must be whole bunch BS. “Thanks Dad!”

Now that I am actually doing it, and I see how important it is for this to be a part of everyone’s life, Now I understand those people really did want to share their knowledge, and why they wanted to share it.

That’s what our events are all about, us sharing our knowledge with you!

We truly do believe real estate investing must become a part of your life, and if you want to know why just ask any million or billionaire. Real estate may not be the way they got there, although it was for many, but it’s in there somewhere and there are very important reasons why it’s in there. Reasons that guess what? Yea, no one’s ever taught you about.

Well we want to be the ones who do. The younger you are the better it is, but it’s never too late. It was 1975 when My dad talked to me about real estate and I’ve only been doing this for 7 years, you do the math. Seriously though, Come to this event!

Just click that button below and see what it’s like to meet people who not only want to share their real estate knowledge with you, but also support each other in achieving their real estate and financial goals.

Boom! There it is! Just click it, I promise you won’t feel a thing, but you’ll be glad you did!
